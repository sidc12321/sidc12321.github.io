Predicting IPL Player Salaries

In this project, I built a machine learning model to predict IPL player salaries based on their past performance statistics. The model used features like:

  • Batting averages and strike rates
  • Bowling economy and wicket-taking rates
  • Player experience and age
  • Past auction prices
  • Recent form and performance trends

I used Python and scikit-learn to build and evaluate several models including random forests, gradient boosting, and neural networks. The final model achieved an R-squared value of 0.82 on the test set.

The model revealed interesting insights about what drives player valuations, like the premium placed on all-rounders and the diminishing returns on experience after age 32. It can help teams make more data-driven decisions in auctions.

I presented this work at a sports analytics conference where it generated interest from several IPL franchises for potential use in their auction strategy.
