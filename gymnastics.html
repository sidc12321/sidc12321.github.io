US Olympic Gymnastics Team Selection

In this Project, I and my teammates build an algorithm to autmatically select the optimal US gymnastics team for the Paris 2024 Olympics. This was submitted to and won the 2024 USOPC (US Olympic and Paralympic Committee) Data Challenge

I presented at the UConn Sports Analytics Symposius and will be presenting soon at the USOPC Performance Innovation Summit to the Selection Committee for future adoption.

The algorithm was able to use a large volume of athlete performance data to determine player strengths for all recent athletes. Then, we would jointly optimize teams over all participating countries to determine which selection of teams would be best. This was achieved through an iterative tree traversal procedure.

The project simulated over 200,000 combinations of teams and determined how good each one was. We optimized with a variety of objective functions, including total number of medals, total gold medals, team/individual medals, etc allowing flexibility. Athletes could also easily be swapped in and out.
