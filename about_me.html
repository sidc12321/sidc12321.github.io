About Me

Hi, my name is Siddharth, I'm currently a data scientist at Bridgewater Associates. Before this, I was an undergraduate and Part III student at the University of Cambridge, where I studied Mathematics, followed by a Master's in Statistics and Data Science at Yale.

I really like working on challenging analytical problems in machine learning from a wide range of domains, and I'm always looking for new opportunities to do so. Some of my recent work has been in prediction markets and opportunities in personal trading. So far, the prospects have been promising! Do let me know if you have interesting ideas here.

For more details on my work, feel free to check out my project portfolio.
